What makes unavailable.it better than the million other similar sites?

Messages are encrypted at rest.

All messages are stored in our database and encrypted with a key that only you have (your url). We don't retain the key for your message. This means that if anybody gains access to our database, whether through hacking, legal supoena, blackmail, etc, then your message is still secure. We couldn't decrypt it even if we wanted to.

It's open source.

Anybody can download the source code and verify it's secure.

Don't trust us with your data? You shouldn't. That's why this site is completely open source including Dockerfiles and build files. All you need is a computer that can serve HTTP and you can run this site on your own.

It's free!

One day we might have a paid "premium" service, but we don't now, and the core service will always be free.

We'll start talking premium services if the site ever becomes a financial burden to maintain.
